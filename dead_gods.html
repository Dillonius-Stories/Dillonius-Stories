Dead Gods

The problem is not the living gods. The problem is the dead gods.

Continents of divinity, untouchable and unusable, falling around you and changing your universe until it is uninhabitable.

You scavenge underneath the corpse for any remenants of what you knew before to survive off of.

The space around you solidifies, restricting you more and more.

The natural processes you knew are warped and different, not enough to kill you, but enough to drive you to desperation and madness.

Patience is no virtue here, the cadaver will not leave, you will not escape
